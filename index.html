Introduction
Based on the target that I want to draw my attention back to the small details of myself rather than trapped in hollow, giant social issues,
I build a dynamic website that visualizes my personal data. The process of this project also wishes to be applied to awaken the sensibility of
the recorded people to the tiny facts in life. In the display section, I wanted to extract the nine days that had changed a lot over the period
and integrate them into one website. Each of the nine grids has a visual display of specific data when clicked on. Each page is shown as a
individual poster as a metaphor of the importance of the individual.
